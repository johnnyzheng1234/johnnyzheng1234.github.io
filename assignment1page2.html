Dogs offer numerous physical and mental health benefits!

Dogs are proven to reduce stress and anxiety, increase physical activity, and boost your overall happiness. Dogs provide unconditional love and loyal companionship, and help to combat loneliness and social isolation.

Teaches responsibility

Caring for a dog teaches children and adults about responsibility, nurturing, and the needs of another living being.

Enhanced social connections

Walking a dog provides opportunities to meet new people, making owners more approachable and fostering new friendships.
